            July 19, 1870.
            Wm. Breeden, Esq.
            Clk. Sup. Ct, Santa Fe, New Mex.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 7th inst, enclosing "certiorari and return, complete transcript of the record and citation in the case of Juan B. Vigil, et al. vs. The U. S.," and to thank you for your promptness in the matter.
            Very Respectfully, &amp;c.   M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clk.
            reception transcript &amp;c. Vigil case.
            see p 353 ante
