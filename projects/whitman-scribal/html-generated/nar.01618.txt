           January 12, 1871.
           Hon. Wilmot W. Brookings,
           Associate Justice of the Supreme Court of Dakota Territory
           Sir:
           Your letter of the 5th instant is received. The leave of absence which you request for thirty days, to commence on the 22d of January, instant, is hereby granted.
           Very respectfully,
           A. T. Akerman,
           Attorney General.
            leave of absence
