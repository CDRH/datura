         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Jesse Conn if he shall be found in the County of
            Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court
            of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of Washington, in the City
            of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> answer the Petition of freedom of Samuel Smith filed in said Court against him.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof, fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness, W. Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 29th day of Jany. 1836</p>
