      <h6 class="fw">20a</h6>
                                               
      
      <div class="dateline">Branson, Mo., April 7, '26. </div>
      
      
      <div class="salute">My dear Mrs. Weston:- </div>
      
      
      <p>Please pardon my delay in replying to yours of March 30th. I have been swamped with
         work and this is my first breathing spell.
      </p>
      
      
      <p>I agree to accept $400 for the four lectures to be given on Dec. 4th and 11th this
         year, as follows:
      </p>
      
      
      <p>Readings from THE SONG OF THE INDIAN WARS, a part of the Epic Cycle of the West.</p>
      
      
      <p>mornings   Dec. 4. "The Beecher's Island Fight".
         Dec. 11. "The Death of Crazy Horse".
      </p>
      
      
      <p>Two Lectures on POETIC VALUES.</p>
      
      
      <p>evenings   Dec. 4. Science and Poetry.
         Dec. 11. The Creative Dream.
      </p>
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">Very truly yours, </div>
         
         
         <br></br>Jno. G. Neihardt
          
      </div>
      
      <p>Later on, I can furnish you material for advertising, if you wish.</p>
      
   </div>
