ST. LOUIS POST-DISPATCH

"OF MAKING MANY BOOKS"

JOHN G. NEIHARDT

BRANSON, MISSOURI
February 14 1 9 3 1
Dr. U. S. Conn Nebraska State Teachers College Wayne, Nebraska
Dear Dr. Conn:

As to compensation for lectures, I seldom receive less than one hundred dollars, but in this case I should like to leave the matter entirely to you and to the various schools. They will know what they can pay and since I intend to be in Nebraska anyway, going nearly as far west as Chadron, traveling expenses may be overlooked.

I don't know where the time has gone since your letter arrived -- a whole week!

With all kind thoughts,


Jno. Neihardt

John G. Neihardt
