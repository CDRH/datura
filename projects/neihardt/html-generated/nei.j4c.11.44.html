1. Mott Collecton U of Mo Library
[1926]
July 26.
Dear Mott:

I've just seen your review of P.V. in The Sat. Rev. of Lit. Bully! You certainly did it generously and I'm grateful. That will help a lot.

You'll be glad to hear that my stuff shows a very considerable increase in sales this year.

Indian Wars

went into a new edition in March - making three, counting the de luxe. The school edition of Friends-Glass

44
-2-

made the biggest advance yet. I'm greatly encouraged.

When are you going to take a vacation here in these beautiful hills with us? You are, of course, invited - you meaning the family too. I have a car & could show you around.

Kindest thoughts!


Jno. Neihardt
