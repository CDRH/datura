      <p>The Nebraska Legislature has passed the bill declaring me laureate, and the Governor
         has signed. Here is the text of the resolution:
      </p>
      
      
      <blockquote>
         <p>
            
            <p>WHEREAS, there is the closest connection between the growth of civilization and the
               development of literature, and
            </p>
            
            
            <p>WHEREAS, wise commonwealths, in all ages, have recognized this relation by lifting
               the poet to the same plane as the statesman and military chieftain, and
            </p>
            
            
            <p>WHEREAS, John G. Neihardt, a citizen of Nebraska, has written a national epic wherein
               he has developed the mood of courage with which our pioneers explored and subdued
               our plains, and thus has inspired in Americans that love of the land and its heroes
               whereby great national traditions are built and perpetuated, and
            </p>
            
            
            <p>WHEREAS, our people wish to exalt such gifts of the human spirit, therefore be it</p>
            
            
            <p>RESOLVED AND ENACTED, by the House of Representatives, the Senate concurring, that
               John G. Neihardt be, and hereby is, declared poet laureate of Nebraska and the prairies."
            </p>
            
         </p>
      </blockquote>
      
      
      
      <p>If you can do anything to aid in circulating this, all of us will be grateful.</p>
      
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">Yours always, </div>
         
         
         <br></br>Jno. N.
          
      </div>
      
      
      
      <p>The chief point to be emphasized is that no other legislative body in America has
         ever done this before.
      </p>
